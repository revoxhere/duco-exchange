Listing criteria

If you want to list a new coin/token/etc. on the DUCO Exchange, make sure you firstly comply with the rules below:

  • 1. Asset must be confirmed not to be a scam.
  • 2. Assets for the exchange will have to be supplied to us by the person creating a listing proposal.
  • 3. Assets that aren't just a lazy fork of something existing and are interesting have a higher priority in listing.

The best way to get listed is to write an e-mail to duino.coin@gmail.com.

In the e-mail, describe the project briefly and we will respond to discuss more details.
Listing is completely free (other than supplying us the exchanged asset).
